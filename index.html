№ 03 — What it is

The instrument is the room.

A live modular electronic set built around a Tesla coil. The music is composed to move a room from stillness to release — and at the peak, the coil sings: a musical arc of lightning the audience can literally take in hand.

Fluorescent tubes are passed into the crowd. Held up to the coil, they light from the current in the air — no wires, no touch. The people holding them stop being an audience and become part of the instrument, a circle of light around the arc. It is closer to ceremony than to a gig.

The set is played on eurorack modular synthesis and hardware — the same rig behind Vox Arboris — with the coil voiced as its own instrument in the mix. Built for festivals, ceremonies, and the kind of late room that wants somewhere to go.

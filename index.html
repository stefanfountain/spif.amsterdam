№ 02 — The ritual

Hands on the lightning.

The instrument is the room.

Put lightning into your crowd's hands. A live melodic DJ set — inspired by the likes of Lane 8, Joachim Pastor and Tō.Arrakis — with a live musical Tesla coil that fires lightning to the beats and tones of the music.

The crowd plays with fluorescent tubes, becoming part of the music: hold one up and it lights, drawing lightning from the coil. Technology done right is indistinguishable from magic — a live musical performance not to miss.

Vox Scintillae

an ayahuasca without ayahuasca

A 30–60 minute hypnotic journey that installs a calibration point you can come back to. Anytime.

The premise

We don't see reality directly. We see models of reality.

These models are necessary. Without them we'd have to figure out what a chair is every time we walked into a room. Models are how we function — they let us recognise, predict, and act.

But some models are wrong. Many are incomplete. The cost of an incomplete model is that we miss things we'd otherwise have lived.

The most important model you carry — and one of the most incomplete — is the model of yourself. We are all a little bit Narcissus, staring at a reflection we can never touch, mistaking the image for the thing.

That's the model we're going to investigate.

The arc isn't discover your true self. The arc is: realise the model is incomplete, find what's missing, hold the result more loosely. Take that suppleness home as a calibration point you can return to.

What gets installed

By the end of the journey, the audience leaves with three things:

  • A direct experience of the gaps in their self-model — moments they'd forgotten about themselves, recovered as evidence the model is incomplete
  • A name for the skill that did the work — suppleness of perspective
  • An anchor — paired with the firing of a Tesla coil — that can re-fire the felt state any time it's encountered again

Bar

Not transformation. A warm reminder of flexibility, calibrated for a public festival setting. The journey is permissive throughout — posture is self-directed, eyes can open or stay closed, the body chooses its own way.

Genre

Ayahuasca without ayahuasca. Music + voice + hypnotic-style journey. The audience never has to dance, never has to speak, never has to perform. They are not the audience. They are the protagonist.
